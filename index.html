HYDRA
SCORE: 0
TIME REMAINING: 30
  1. Hydra heads will appear and start changing color.
  2. Click on the heads when they are blue to cut them off.
  3. Once a head has changed color, you will have to get it back to blue by clicking to change it back to its previous color before you can cut it off.
  4. Once a head reaches red, it will spew fire and end the game.
  5. Oh and... once you cut off a Hydra's head, another two will grow in its place. Good Luck!
