DIFFUSE
SCORE: 0
LEVEL: 1
TIME REMAINING: 30
  1. Bombs will appear and start changing color.
  2. Click on the bombs when they are green to remove them.
  3. Once a bomb has changed color, you will have to get it back to green by clicking to change it back to its previous color before you can remove it.
  4. Once a bomb reaches red, it will explode and end the game.
